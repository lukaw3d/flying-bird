Flappy Bird

Problem Statement

Your task is to implement the popular game Flappy Bird. There are no hard constraints on how you design and build your solution, but you will be scored primarily based on functionality, and then secondarily on user experience.

Before you begin your implementation, you may want to design the game state you want to maintain, and how to update it appropriately to yield the desired behavior.

Tools

Feel free to pick a programming language/framework of your choice. You are free to use the Internet for developer documentation as necessary to accomplish your task. Please do not copy-paste any code you find on the Internet.

Submission

Please submit a tarball/zip of your complete code. Please include a short writeup of components you were unable to complete, known bugs you didn’t have time to address, etc. This write-up will be a crucial part of your score.
